# Design Contract

The target site `https://www.seniorgaps.com/` is used only as a structural reference.

Senior Equity Alliance must have original branding, original copy, original layout refinements, original colors, and an original hero treatment.

Do not copy SeniorGaps logos, exact wording, exact images, footer text, testimonials, partner logos, or contact details.

The website should communicate a senior-focused, trustworthy, calm, professional, real-estate and equity assistance brand.

Visual tone: trustworthy, warm, dignified, clear, senior-friendly, and accessible.

The design should feel similar in broad section flow to the reference but not identical.

Use responsive design for mobile, tablet, and desktop.

Use semantic HTML, accessible form labels, high contrast text, large readable type, and clear calls to action.

Avoid dark patterns, pressure tactics, exaggerated financial claims, or predatory language.

The website should feel like an ethical senior advocacy and property-equity guidance organization.
